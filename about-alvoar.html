About Us
Welcome to Alvoar Lacteos Sa Global, your premier diary farm food produce & beverage distributor headquartered in Malaysia. We take pride in offering an extensive range of quality products coupled with efficient delivery services tailored to meet your needs. With a minimum order quantity of RM300, we ensure free delivery within the KLANG VALLEY and KUALA LUMPUR area, making it convenient for you to access our diverse selection of items.

At Alvoar Lacteos Sa Global, we understand the varied requirements of our clients. That's why most of our products, including coffee beans, tea, syrups, powders, Halal food diary essentials like butter and cheese, as well as pantry staples like spaghetti, cream and butter, can be purchased individually - eliminating the need to buy entire cartons. Whether you seek specific ingredients or disposable products, we've got you covered. Our commitment to prompt service means that once your order is received, expect swift delivery within 1 to 2 working days, ensuring your kitchen is always well-stocked and ready for action. Experience convenience, reliability, and quality with Alvoar Lacteos Sa Global - your trusted partner in the food & beverage distribution industry.

Our aim is delivering outstanding and reliable services and dealing with integrity, for your business success and satisfaction of your customers.

Explore a complete range of food and beverage supplies, dairy products, bakery ingredient,and disposable kitchenware with us, serving Selangor, KL, and throughout Malaysia with export capabilities to meet our customers satisfaction.

As a diary food supplier in Kuala Lumpur that emphasises freshness, we are confident in our service to supply fresh farm produce and goods to our customers. We do quality control checks before delivering your orders but if you are unsatisfied with the delivered goods due to reason such as spoilage, we will replace your order with no charge.
